About CipherMaster

CipherMaster started as a fun side project to explore how classic encryption techniques like Caesar, ROT13, and Morse code work. Over time, it grew into a fully functional and stylish terminal-based utility with multiple cipher systems, clipboard integration, and even its own unique encoding format.
